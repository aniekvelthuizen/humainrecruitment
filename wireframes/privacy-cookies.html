Privacy & Cookies

How HumAIn Recruitment handles your personal data and uses cookies. We keep it plain English — no legal maze.

Last updated: [date] · Applies to: humainrecruitment.com

Who we are

HumAIn Recruitment B.V. ("HumAIn Recruitment", "we", "us") is the controller of the personal data described in this policy. Our registered address and contact details are listed in the Contact section below.

In plain terms: we're a recruitment agency. We handle data about candidates (job seekers) and contacts at client companies. This page tells you exactly what we collect, why, and what you can do about it.

What we collect

Candidates

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • CV and application documents
  • Work history, skills, and preferences you share with us
  • Communication history (emails, call notes)
  • Any additional info you provide during the process

Employers / contacts

  • Name, job title, company, and business contact details
  • Communication history

Website visitors

  • Usage data via analytics (pages visited, session duration)
  • Data submitted via contact or application forms
  • Technical data (browser type, IP address — anonymised where possible)

  • We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Your rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we process your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@humainrecruitment.com. We respond within 30 days.
